Here is a step-by-step guide on how to do a full-highlight! It should look something like this.

First things first!

Obviously before you start . you'll need to know what tools and products you'll need.

Your Tools!

  • A client or model
  • Towels
  • Cape
  • Combs
  • Clips
  • Foils
  • Color Brush

Your Products!

DISCLAIMER:The addition of a star means product is not required but highly reccomended.

  • Lightener Reccomended
  • Developer Reccomended
  • Bond Rebuilder* Must be purchased from a salon supply store like Cosmoprof or Salon Centric
  • Toner Recccomended

Your Steps!

  1. Create a 5 sections, two in the back, two on the sides, and a mohawk on top.
  2. From here begin in the parting of your choosing and prepare to foil
  3. Follow a pattern of slicing, weaving, and teasing to achieve a heavy highlight. Note that the more foils you place the more blond you will achieve.
  4. Continue this pattern throughout each section
  5. Once you have completed your foiling, check the sections you started first to make sure you dont over process.
  6. If your first sections are processed you can rinse only these partings, but make sure you have allowed your hair to process to at least the inside of a banana peel.
  7. Once all sections have processed you can rinse and prepare to tone
  8. Once you have mixed yout toner you can apply it for the manufactors reccomended amount of time
  9. Rinse the toner, shampoo, and condition or apply a mask
  10. Take your clinet or model back to your station and begin to dry and style their hair
  11. Send your client home with a big smile and tons of confidence!

Good Job!! Have Fun!!
